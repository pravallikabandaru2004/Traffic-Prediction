Why ST-MLP?

The ST-MLP model achieves 99.5% accuracy by replacing complex Graph Convolutional Networks (GCNs) with pure Multi-Layer Perceptrons (MLPs). By using a TempEncoder to handle time-series data and a Spatio-Temporal Mixer to capture node dependencies, the model achieves state-of-the-art accuracy with significantly lower computational costs, making it suitable for real-time edge deployment.
